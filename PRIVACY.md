# Privacy Policy for PageTalk

**Last updated: March 4, 2026**

## Overview

PageTalk ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that lets users post and read comments attached to any webpage URL. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

## Data We Collect

### Data you provide
- **Comments and replies** you write and submit through the Extension.
- **Display name** you optionally set in the Extension's Settings panel.

### Data generated automatically
- **Anonymous user ID** — a randomly generated identifier created locally on your device the first time you use the Extension. This ID is never linked to your name, email, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information. It is stored only in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

### Data we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
- We do not collect your browsing history or track which pages you visit beyond the URL of the page you are currently viewing when you open the Extension popup.
- We do not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any cross-site tracking.
- We do not collect analytics or telemetry.

## How Data Is Used

- Comments you post, your display name, and your anonymous user ID are stored in a shared database (Supabase) so that other users visiting the same URL can read and respond to them.
- The current page's URL is read solely to look up or create the discussion thread for that page. It is not logged or stored independently of the comment threads you participate in.
- Your vote history (upvotes/downvotes) is stored locally on your device in `chrome.storage.local` and in the shared database linked only to your anonymous user ID.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.
- Comment data is stored with [Supabase](https://supabase.com), our database provider. Their privacy policy applies to data at rest on their platform.
- Because comments are public and tied only to an anonymous ID, no personally identifiable information is shared with any party.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Comments and votes are stored in the shared database indefinitely so that discussions remain available to future visitors of the same page.

If you wish to delete your data, contact us at the email below and provide your anonymous user ID (visible by opening the Extension's settings and copying your User ID). We will delete all comments and votes associated with that ID.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data through the Extension, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
